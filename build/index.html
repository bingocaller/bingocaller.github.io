Leisure

I’m a 28-year old Danish guy who lives and works in Copenhagen. Right now I spend most of my free time with my wife and my daughter, but when I’m not with them, I enjoy playing video games and hanging out with my friends.

I like to read about the technologies I spend my daily life working with. I’m always seeking out new knowledge on sites like Smashing Magazine and A List Apart. One of my favourite things is finding an interesting blog post or article, send it to my Kindle and read it later at my own leisure.

Recently I've also gotten really hooked on podcasts. I think it's a brilliant way of absorbing knowledge while doing other things, like getting from A to B, or even while working.
For front-end related stuff, I enjoy listening to Full Stack Radio and Shop Talk Show. Some of my non-technical favourites include: The Nerdist, Serial (of course), The Black Tapes, Lore, Fat Man on Batman, and How Did This Get Made?

I love coffee. I’m not particularly picky when it comes to just a regular cup of joe, but when I’m at home with my own equipment I like to take my time to really try to get that perfect cup. For the time being I’m very pleased with the results I get from grinding my own beans and brewing in a stovetop Bialetti Moka Express. If I’m making coffee for more people I use my french press. I really wanna try an AeroPress®, though!
